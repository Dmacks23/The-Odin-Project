The Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the quintessential comfort food, a baked Italian dish that layers sheets of tender pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese. Each bite offers a delightful harmony of flavors and textures, from the savory, tomato-infused meat sauce to the luscious, velvety béchamel that blankets every layer. The golden, bubbly cheese crust on top adds a delightful crunch, making lasagna a feast for both the eyes and the palate. Perfect for family gatherings or a cozy night in, this dish epitomizes home-cooked goodness.

Ingredients

The Meat Sauce

  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 small can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

The Cheese Layer

  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

The Pasta

  • 12 lasagna noodles (uncooked or pre-cooked, based on your preference)

The Garnish

  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. Prepare the Meat
    • In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned.
    • Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook until the onion is soft and translucent.
    • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Add the sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
    • Simmer the sauce on low heat for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Cook the Noodles if needed
    • If using traditional lasagna noodles, cook them according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Mix the Cheese Layer
    • In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and salt. Mix until smooth.
  4. Preheat the Oven
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F
  5. Assemble the Lasagna
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta.
    • Repeat the layers, starting with the meat sauce, until you run out of ingredients, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
    • Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the final layer of meat sauce.
  6. Bake the Lasagna
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let It Rest
    • Allow the lasagna to cool for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving. This helps the layers set and makes it easier to cut.
  8. Garnish and Serve
    • Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired, and serve hot.
